Problem/Opportunity

As part of my Accessible Design course at SVSU (RPW * 324), I conducted and wrote this Design Assessment, which required me to describe and evaluate a design (i.e., an object or process).

Tools

I created this Assessment with Adobe InDesign.

Solution

I assessed the design of WCONLINE®, the online platform the SVSU Writing Center uses to schedule and conduct online tutorial sessions (and an “object” I used multiple times per week in my role as a tutor). With this project, I was able to critically assess how the platform’s design sought to support its function for use by users with a variety of abilities, sparking my interest in accessibility.
